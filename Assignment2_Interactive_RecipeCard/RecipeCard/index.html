🍫 Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 mins

Serves: 6 people

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs and milk, stir until smooth.
  4. Pour into a baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Cool and serve with chocolate frosting.
